Mission: Aurora

Experience in 2073

What is in the experience?

The "Mission: Aurora experience in 2073” involves riding the CK-391 detachable airship, equipped with on-board multilingual voice translation, through a dome-shaped indoor game center with holographic screens and holographic projection drones.

After boarding and departing, the airship will separate into three separate aircraft, with two aircraft following route 1 and the other following route 2. During the journey, unmanned holographic drones will be projected to tell the story of the mission and provid real-time projections of the other airships playing on the same course.
