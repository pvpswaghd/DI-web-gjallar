History

"On April 11th, 2113, coinciding with the 40th anniversary of Gjallar, the organization will set up amusement parks around the world with the main goal of: allowing modern humans to learn about past history and reminding humanity not to repeat the same mistakes. Celebrating the completion stage of the Earth's recovery. After experiencing the shadow of the cold winter, human society has finally united sincerely and treated others equally."
