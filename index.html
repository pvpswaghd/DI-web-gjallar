About Project Gjallar

History

On April 11, 2113, coinciding with the 40th anniversary of "Gjallar", the organization will set up amusement parks around the world with the goal of:

1. Allowing modern humans to understand history and reminding them not to repeat past mistakes.

2. Celebrating the completion of Earth's recovery efforts.

3. Allowing human society to unite and treat others sincerely and equally, after experiencing the shadow of the Fimbulwinter.

In this 40th Anniversary, not only will we be in memorial of Prof. Oliversum.

2030
The Earth's population expanded unexpectedly and faced rapid depletion of various resources, and extreme weather conditions, resulting in food crises, species extinction, cultural fractures, and other health problems.
At this time, governments around the world still insisted on promoting environmental policies of "relief and adaptation," but Mr. Oliviersum decided to take preparatory action to respond to the imminent crisis because he thought that it was a matter of survival.
Mr. Oliviersum invested the family's assets and secretly recruited many scholars and scientists to launch the ‘Fimbulwinter’ project, which was later also referred to as the 'Harbinger of Doomsday' project.

Project Fimbulwinter
Project Fimbulwinter is the predecessor of Project Gjallar. It aimed to make every effort to solve or prevent crises that could lead to the extinction of society through research and technology development. Help humanity survive the three upcoming ‘Fimbulwinter.’
