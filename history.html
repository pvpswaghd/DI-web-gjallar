Founder

Yuri Gagarin Oliviersum

Mr Oliviersum is the founder of Project Gjallar, which was originally named Project 'Fimbulwinter'. He was the leader of the project, and contributed to humanity in extreme conditions.



History

  • ~2030

    Phase 1

    The Earth's population expanded unexpectedly and faced rapid depletion of various resources, and extreme weather conditions, resulting in food crises, species extinction, cultural fractures, and other health problems. At this time, governments around the world still insisted on promoting environmental policies of "relief and adaptation," but Mr. Oliviersum decided to take preparatory action to respond to the imminent crisis because he thought that it was a matter of survival.

  • Project 'Fimbulwinter'


    Mr. Oliviersum invested the family's assets and secretly recruited many scholars and scientists to launch the ‘Fimbulwinter’ project, which was later also referred to as the 'Harbinger of Doomsday' project.



    Project 'Fimbulwinter' aims to do the following:


    1. Hope to make every effort to solve or prevent crises that could lead to the extinction of society through research and technology development.


    2. Help humanity survive the three upcoming ‘Fimbulwinter.’

  • 2040-2050

    Phase 2 - The First Winter

    During the 'First winter' period, while governments around the world continued to vigorously promote and implement various environmental measures, extreme weather conditions continued to intensify at an incredible pace. In most areas, daytime temperatures in summer reached 60 degrees Celsius, and the summer season extended to around six months. The sea ice in both polar regions was almost completely melting, and natural disasters such as wildfires and typhoons occurred frequently.

  • Base establishment

    The 'Fimbulwinter' self-financed rescue team went to various parts of the world to aid refugees and set up shelters. At the same time, to prevent further intensification and worsening of extreme weather conditions, Project 'Fimbulwinter' established bases in the North and South Poles to artificially produce ice and slow down the melting rate of sea ice, to prevent the continued deterioration of the effects of global warming.

  • 2050-2060

    Phase 3 - The Second Winter

    During the 'Second winter' period, although the impact of the 'First winter' was controlled and did not intensify thanks to the efforts of the organization, the destruction caused by the extreme climate conditions prior to it brought about the 'Second winter'. The heatwaves caused farmlands to burn, along with the effects of other extreme weather, resulting in three core impacts of the Second winter: a food crisis caused by shortages, destruction and loss of historical buildings and cultural relics, and the extinction of animals and plants in extreme environments.

  • Concentrated Food

    A picture containing text, empty Description automatically generatedTo check the food crisis, Mr. Oliviersum developed concentrated food and distributed it for free to refugees in shelters. Although the taste and texture of this food were not great, it had a long shelf life, low cost, and was nutritious.

  • First-generation 'seeds'

    In addition, to prevent the continued loss and destruction of various surviving historical relics, the organization's researchers worked hard during this period to collect and produce all kinds of 'seeds'. These 'seeds' replicated and backed up various human knowledge, data, cultural relics, and genetic information of various animals and plants.

  • 2060-2080

    Phase 4 - The 'Third Winter'

    The 'Third Winter' period was the most difficult and chaotic time. In addition to the follow-up effects of the 'First Winter' and 'Second Winter', human society also faced the depletion of various resources such as clean water, clean air, and fossil fuels. People in various shelters were about to face energy shortages and health threats. People around the world were in a panic, and conflicts and disputes were endless.

  • Quote from Mr. Oliviersum

    At this critical moment when human society was about to collapse, Mr. Oliviersum stepped forward to unite everyone and work together to get through the harsh winter.


    「We are in this together. And together, we will overcome this.」


    As the organization was about to exhaust its resources and properties, under the direct command of Mr. Oliviersum, the organization would make a desperate attempt to send an exploration team to outer space to seek technological breakthroughs and solve the end-of-the-world crisis on Earth.

  • 2070

    Foundation of a space station

    In 2070, the organization began to build a space station in outer space, concentrating the most advanced equipment and resources on the station, focusing efforts on researching new breakthroughs, and backing up the seeds in a safe place.

  • July 21, 2073

    Mission: Aurora

    The elite team selected by Mr. Oliviersum himself set off into outer space to carry out the 'Mission: Aurora', seeking a glimmer of hope for saving the human world in the 'Fimbulwinter' of humanity. After entrusting the exploration team with the important mission, Mr. Oliviersum stayed on Earth to oversee the overall situation.

  • 2078

    Phase 5 - Enfilitus

    In 2078, after five years of exploration, the exploration team brought back core technology from the planet K-452b. Later, despite opposition from others, the elderly Mr. Oliviersum led the research team day and night in the half-finished space station to study the Enfilitus crystal brought back by the exploration team. Ultimately, through their efforts, the organization successfully developed new clean energy using the Enfilitus crystal and advanced facilities on the space station, and replaced the power supply facilities on Earth in a short period of time, solving the energy crisis.

  • 2078

    Phase 6 - Memorial and Bond

    On March 14, 2080, Mr. Oliviersum, who was overwhelmed and overworked, passed away shortly after witnessing the birth of the new energy. But under the leadership of Mr. Oliviersum, human society finally found a glimmer of hope in the 'Fimbulwinter'. After experiencing this, the barriers between people gradually became smaller, and people tended to cooperate and trust each other more than ever before.

  • 2080 - 2085

    Project Gjallar

    "On April 11, 2083, the space station was completed, and the organization's scientists, researchers, and their families were invited to live on the space station to focus on researching the Earth's recovery work and continuing to pass on Mr. Oliversum's ideas. To officially declare that humanity has successfully passed the "Fimbulwinter" and ushered in a new beginning, and to warn humanity not to repeat the mistakes of the past, the organization was officially renamed "Gjallar"."
